Paintings

One of the things I've been doing since I was a little kid is painting. I remember sitting at the table with a large sheet of paper and some paint in front of me. I immediately started painting and had a blast. When I was done and the paint dried, I was always really proud of myself. I still enjoy painting, but I do it less these days. I can really sit in my room for hours and work on a painting. Sometimes I don't even realize that I have been working for almost 4 hours. I just really wanted to finish my painting and see the end result. Below you can see some paintings I made.
