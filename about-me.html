About me

Hi! My name is Zara and I am 17 years old. In my spare time I love to make art. I created this website because I want to share my art with other people. My art style varies quite a bit, but the one thing I draw the most is objects or plants with faces on them. I enjoy drawing these the most because they aren’t realistic and I think they look funny. I usually use bright colors, but I sometimes also work with just black fineliner. When I'm drawing or painting, it's really a moment of rest for me. I’ll put on some music and I can work on a drawing or painting for hours. The same goes for when I do my makeup. I also put some makeup looks I created on this website, because I think that is also a form of art. I will tell you more about this under the heading “Paintings and Make-up”. I hope you like this site and my work and have fun looking and reading!
